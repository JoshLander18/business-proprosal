Business Proposal

Feasibility Analysis

Product / Service

After researching it can be seen that there is an ever growing amount of businesses which need clothing to solidify their brand and service and a large number of individuals who want to commemorate important events in their lives with uniformed clothing. Everyone has their own personal style; unfortunately, the execution by other companies may not transfer the customers' ideas efficiently. Custom Junction allows customers to express themselves and work hand in hand with our team members to properly make their ideas wearable and affordable. Our team assists customers with every detail throughout the process of the customization of their clothing choice to have an easy and stress-free experience.

Additionally, feedback from potential customers is welcomed to help improve our services going forward. If trends appear within the industry we will be able to react quickly and offer new clothing options to suit. With our affordable prices and welcoming customer service Custom Junction is the solution to all those who want their visions to unfold the way they want.

Industry / Target Market

Industry - The manufacturing of customized clothing is a growing yet saturated industry, especially for T-shirts. T-shirts are wardrobe staples and the personalization adds uniqueness to the item. However, the quality and variety of the items and the service provided is what sets companies apart. Regularly this would deter a business but Custom Junction not only produces T-shirts and a variety of other clothing items.

Target market - Custom Junction will be able to cater to men and women of all ages who are creatives and want to express this in their wardrobe. As well as to other businesses who require that professional touch by adding custom apparel to their brand. This makes it so all who seek our product will surely find something suiting their needs.

Concept Statement

Our business is named Custom Junction, a clothing customization business which plans to work first locally and aims to go regional in future. The premise is that you can order customized clothing to be made or bring in your own items to be customized. Our intended target market will be the general public, but we can cater to businesses as well. Our service aims to get your own ideas brought to life at quick rates and affordable prices.It also solves the problem of not being able to find items that fit your aesthetic by letting us use your style as inspiration for our brand. Compared to our competitors, we will be able to provide easy access on the web, excellent customer service, affordable prices and quick service with our very efficient pickup and drop off system.

BUSINESS MODEL:

STANDARD
DISRUPTIVE

Custom Junction adopts the standard business model. A standard business aims to build upon an already existing approach to run and bring value to your business. This suits our business as we intend to provide the service of customizing a variety of apparel which suit the customers needs. This service aims to work with all clientele from businesses to personal pieces.Custom Junction works within the manufacturer/retailer business model similar to that of Apple. This is a model which produces and sells their products with both offline and online alternatives. Apple provides services and products related to the manufacturing and sale of technology but Custom Junction will allow the customer to have the choice to bring their own attire to be customized or have us make the clothing and customize it.

INDUSTRY & COMPETITOR ANALYSIS

Is the industry a realistic place for our new venture to enter?

Yes, while there are several clothing stores in the local community most do not offer the service that our business would offer to our customers. (personal customizations)

If we do enter the industry, can our firm do a better job than the industry as a whole in avoiding or diminishing the impact of the forces that suppress industry profitability?

Our firm can compete with the industry by utilizing good customer service, providing delivery services and most importantly having a website where customers can conveniently view products from the comfort of their own home.

Is there a unique position in the industry that avoids or diminishes the forces that suppress industry profitability?

Unlike our competitors, our firm would allow customers to view and purchase products through our website rather than being forced to visit our store in person. By removing this time commitment, we provide a more convenient shopping experience.

Recently the clothing industry was impacted negatively because of supply chain challenges experienced by raw material suppliers in North America due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Custom Junction decided it was a great opportunity to venture into sourcing soft sheer fabrics from regional suppliers to address the need for a specific type of custom-made shirt that is more appropriate for our tropical climate. From research conducted we recognized that Guyana was open, and not locked down like its neighbors. After approaching Guyana the fabric makers were willing to supply our company with Muslin, and other cotton based fabrics for our custom-made shirts. The trade agreement was drawn up to lock Guyana and Custom Junction into a 5-year trade agreement, to not supply any of our competitors with those select types of fabrics. Our competitors at the time were selling t-shirts made of thick jersey fabric.
